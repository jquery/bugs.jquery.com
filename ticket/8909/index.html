$(element).data() will scan all attributes more than needed.

Reported by: gnarf Owned by: gnarf
Priority: blocker Milestone: 1.7
Component: data Version: 1.5.2
Keywords: 1.7-discuss Cc:
Blocked by: Blocking:
Description

If you make more than one call to 

$(element).data()
you end up having to loop over the attributes of the element looking for "data-" attributes every time you access it. I'd like to propose adding a simple flag in our private 
_data()
that can stop us from having to check more than once...
