Thanks for submitting a ticket to the jQuery project. Under the testing conditions described, I haven't been able to reproduce the error experienced with jQuery.com and http://www.google-analytics.com/ga.js with OSX or Windows.

Please also note that with respect to the rounded corners on our site not appearing in IE6-8, our design team design with graceful degradation in mind. This means that modern browsers which support CSS3 features such as border-radius are able to get an experience supporting 'modern' rendering of corners using CSS3, whilst older browsers will fallback to standard edges instead.

That said, I've just double checked jQuery.com rendering with IE8 in a VM and the corners appear to be showing up fine regardless of browser.
